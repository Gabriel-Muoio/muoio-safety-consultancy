Practice

Muoio Safety Consultancy works with organisations operating in high-risk environments to strengthen how critical risks are understood, governed, and controlled. The consultancy supports leadership teams in the development and review of safety management systems, examination of critical risk controls, and investigation of serious incidents to identify systemic causes and guide organisational response.

About

Muoio Safety Consultancy is operated by Gabriel Muoio, who holds an Advanced Diploma of Work Health and Safety as well as a Graduate Certificate in Occupational Health and Safety from Curtin University. His work focuses on risk management, safety systems, and safety governance within high-risk industries across Western Australia.
